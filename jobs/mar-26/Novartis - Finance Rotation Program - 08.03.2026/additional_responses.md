# Novartis Finance Rotation Program - Additional Responses

## Question 1: Interest in Program & Experience

**What sparks your interest in the program? What type of experience do you wish to gain? And what do you think you could bring to the program?**

I'm drawn to Novartis's Finance Rotation Program because it offers structured exposure to how financial strategy directly enables pharmaceutical innovation and development of life-changing medicines. I want to develop deep expertise in healthcare finance, understand how to allocate capital effectively to accelerate R&D, and bring my analytical rigor, cross-functional experience, and international perspective to support Novartis's global operations.

---

## Question 2: Contributing to Novartis's Mission

**If selected, how would you contribute to Novartis' purpose of reimagining medicine? What are your ideas and possible actions to support our mission?**

I would contribute to reimagining medicine by treating finance as a strategic enabler—using rigorous analysis to ensure capital flows to the highest-impact therapeutic areas and helping Novartis make data-driven decisions that accelerate time-to-market for breakthrough therapies. My concrete actions would include identifying cost efficiencies in operations to free up resources for R&D investment, building analytical tools to forecast portfolio performance, and supporting cross-functional teams in balancing innovation with financial discipline.
