# Tavily Research: RWE Supply & Trading

## Company Mission & Values

**Core Values/Culture:**
- **Inclusive culture**: "the energy to be inclusive and believe diversity drives success"
- **Bring your whole self to work**: Emphasis on being respected for different opinions, strengths, and experiences
- **Community of support**: Strong team-based environment with 2,200-strong team from over 90 countries
- **Global presence**: Operates Europe's largest and most advanced energy trading floor

**What They're Known For:**
- Europe's largest and most advanced energy trading floor (Essen, Germany)
- Trading mix: electricity, gas, commodities, CO2 emission allowances
- Commercial optimization of power plants' dispatch
- Renewables electricity sales

**Graduate Programme Culture:**
- Emphasis on bringing whole self to work
- Strengths-based approach
- Community support with diverse, international team
- Real project ownership (not typical grad work)

## Recent Company News (2025)

**Key Initiatives:**

1. **Offshore Wind Expansion (Aug 2025):**
   - RWE and North Star signed long-term agreements for next-generation service operation vessels (SOVs)
   - Supporting RWE's **growing offshore wind fleet**
   - Demonstrates commitment to renewable energy infrastructure

2. **Denmark's Largest Offshore Wind Farm (Oct 2024 - ongoing 2025):**
   - Received permits to build Denmark's largest offshore wind farm: **Thor project (1.1 GW)**
   - Offshore construction beginning in 2025
   - Already operates 19 offshore wind farms

3. **New York Offshore Wind (Oct 2024):**
   - RWE and National Grid proposed **2.8 GW wind park** off Long Island
   - Largest offshore wind power plan submitted to New York state

4. **Grid Investment (Sept 2025):**
   - Apollo Global Management agreed to contribute 3.2 billion euros for future power-grid upgrades
   - 25.1% stake in German transmission system operator Amprion

**Strategic Direction:**
- Major expansion in offshore wind capacity across Europe and US
- Growing renewable energy portfolio
- Significant infrastructure investment in power grids
- Positioning as renewable energy leader while maintaining trading expertise
