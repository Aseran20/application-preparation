About the job
The analyst will support the Investment team across private markets funds and deals analysis, pipeline management, industry research, monitoring and analysis of market trends, and preparation of investment reports. The analyst will report to the Portfolio Manager of the Syz Private Market Opportunities (SPMO) program and assist in all aspects of portfolio management, including valuation, reporting, acquisitions, build-ups, liquidity events and exits, strategic development, and operational planning. The analyst will work closely with and alongside the senior management and analysts of the firm, and portfolio investments. The individual will also help in value creation efforts at portfolio and firm level such as automation of internal reports, maintenance of the fund's databases, and administrative support in daily tasks.

What We Offer

Broad exposure to Private Equity, with a focus on European Lower Mid-Market buyout
Hands-on involvement across the full investment cycle: sourcing, underwriting, execution, and monitoring
Early responsibility, steep professional learning curve and fast career advancement
Entrepreneurial environment with flat hierarchies and direct exposure to senior team members
International, dynamic, and close-knit team culture
Regular team-building events

Key responsibilities

Review, screen, and track incoming investment opportunities while maintaining the investment database (pipeline management)
Support the investment team to source deals in specific regions
Support due diligence processes on co-investments, fund investments, and secondaries, including qualitative and quantitative analysis
Support with production of various investment due diligence reports and investor memorandums
Conduct market and industry research and thematic analyses to identify attractive sectors and opportunities, including value chain analysis
Build and maintain financial models and perform Excel-based analyses to support investment decisions
Assist in monitoring and managing portfolio companies and fund positions
Prepare materials for the Investment Committee
Prepare pitchbooks
Draft quarterly reports, with comprehensive operational and valuation report

Your profile

Master’s degree from a leading university in Economics, Finance, Business Administration, Engineering, or a related field
1–3 years of relevant experience in Private Equity, M&A, Transaction Services, or Strategy Consulting (internships included).
Strong analytical and financial modelling skills and business planning, with high attention to detail and problem-solving skills
Proactive, entrepreneurial and analytical mindset with the ability to take initiative and work independently
Extensive experience generating high quality presentations
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a collaborative approach, showing flexibility
Proficiency in Excel, PowerPoint, and Word
Fluent in English and at least one additional European language
