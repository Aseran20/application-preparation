# Glencore Research - Cover Letter Development

## Company Mission & Core Values

**Mission Statement:**
"Advancing everyday life by supplying the commodities needed to develop, sustain and improve the world around us."

**Core Values (6 values):**
1. **Safety**
2. **Integrity**
3. **Simplicity**
4. **Responsibility**
5. **Openness**
6. **Entrepreneurialism** ← Key differentiator

**Culture:**
- Values-driven organization: "at the heart of our culture, the way we do business and the way we work"
- Entrepreneurialism is a core pillar (alongside safety, integrity, responsibility, openness, simplicity)
- Focus on creating value and finding better, safer ways of working
- Long-term growth and opportunity for all stakeholders

## Recent News & Strategic Direction (2025)

**Copper Production Strategy:**
- **Major goal: Return to 1 million tons of copper annual production by 2028**
- Improving existing mines to increase output
- Filed applications for $13 billion of new copper projects in Argentina
- Challenge: Need to produce ~50% more copper in H2 2025 vs H1 2025 to meet annual targets
- Context: Copper critical for energy transition and decarbonization

**Trading Operations:**
- Senior trader movements (oil/gas, metals) - competitive talent market
- Remains one of world's largest commodity traders
- Global presence: North America's largest processor of electronic scrap (Horne Smelter, Quebec)

**Sustainability Focus:**
- Critical minerals processing and recycling initiatives
- Former head of recycling launched minerals processing firm (shows company's expertise in this area)
- Focus on Western minerals processing capabilities

## Key Insights for Cover Letter

**What to reference:**
1. **Core value of entrepreneurialism** - exact wording from their values
2. **1 million ton copper target by 2028** - shows strategic ambition and growth
3. **Individual responsibility from day one** - mentioned in job description, aligns with entrepreneurial culture
4. **Advancing everyday life through commodities** - purpose-driven mission

**Tone:**
- Action-oriented, entrepreneurial, resilient
- Focus on commercial execution and results
- Emphasize ability to handle responsibility independently
