About the job
What It's About

The Partners Group Financial Analyst Program is a unique opportunity for recent university graduates with strong credentials looking to start an exciting and challenging career as an investment professional with a world-leading private markets investment firm.

 The investment track of the program will allow Financial Analysts to experience year-long rotations among a subset of Partners Group's five asset classes: Private Equity, Private Debt, Private Infrastructure, Private Real Estate, and Private Markets Royalties. A Client Solutions and Portfolio Management track will also be available with more information to follow 
 Analysts will gain valuable exposure working in collaboration with various teams across the firm globally 
 Analysts will go through a formal four-week training program, and gain hands-on experience through the entire investment process before starting their first rotation 

Successful candidates will be required to complete a 2/3-year commitment in the program and will gain the financial and analytical skills needed to kick-start their career in private markets investing. Upon completion of 2-3 rotations, analysts will permanently join one of the teams they rotated with. While the first rotation will be based in our global headquarter in Zug, further rotations are not geographically restricted and, subject to business needs, can also be based in our European offices in London, Munich, and Paris.

What We Expect

 Outstanding academic credentials 
 Strong analytical and quantitative skills 
 Independent thinking with problem-solving mindset 
 Excellent interpersonal and written communication, combined with a strong work ethic and professional demeanor 
 Relevant internship experience (in the financial industry and/or elsewhere) 
 A collaborative team-player attitude 

What We Offer

Partners Group is a global financial institution. As a growing firm, we are committed to attracting, developing and retaining the very best talent, by offering a workplace where results are truly recognized and rewarded. We offer an opportunity for you to grow:

 Professional, international working environment 
 Challenging, rewarding career within a growing company 
 Collaborative environment, with on-the-job training and mentorship opportunities 
 Competitive compensation package, including performance-based annual incentives 
 One-month sabbatical after every five years of employment 

Who Can Apply

The Financial Analyst Program is open to students in the final year of their undergraduate and graduate level studies who have achieved top marks in their discipline. We will accept candidates from any field of study, as long as they can demonstrate a genuine interest in private markets and will consider profiles of candidates with up to 1 year of relevant full-time working experience.

As an equal opportunity employer, we welcome diversity in our workplace and do not discriminate against applicants on the basis of age, gender, nationality, or any other basis that is inconsistent with our guiding values. We search for applicants within a global talent pool and look for open-minded team players who possess determination, resilience, effective communication skills, and the ability to inspire others and think outside the box.

We look forward to receiving your application!
