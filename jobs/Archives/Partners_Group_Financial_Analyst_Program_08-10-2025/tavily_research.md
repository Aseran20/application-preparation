# Tavily Research - Partners Group

## Company Overview
- **One of the largest firms in the global private markets industry**
- ~2,000 professionals globally
- Over USD 174 billion in assets under management
- Investment programs across: private equity, private credit, infrastructure, real estate, and royalties
- **Heritage in Switzerland, primary presence in Colorado**
- Listed on SIX Swiss Exchange (2006)

## Core Values & Culture
- **"Built differently from the rest of the industry"** - emphasizes differentiated culture
- **Operationally oriented approach** - focus on identifying attractive investment themes and building businesses into market leaders
- Commitment to high standards of corporate governance
- Transformational investing approach
