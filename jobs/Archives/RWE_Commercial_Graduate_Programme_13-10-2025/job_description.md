Overview of programme
Ready to learn everything there is to know about working at a leading European energy trading house?

From here, you’ll familiarise yourself with both financial and physical energy trading and get an understanding of how RWE operates as a company. You’ll be given significant exposure to all aspects of our value chain and get a taste of power plant optimisation, gas procurement or risk management.

Development & training 
This strengths-based programme is designed specifically for graduates, letting you shape your own path and experience personalised learning in immersive work environments. You’ll find a mixture of online education, face-to-face workshops, on-the-job training and networking events where you will meet senior managers and the Board.

Your mentor and assigned buddy can help advise you on rotations and introduce you to industry experts so that you can explore all aspects of the energy value chain.

Why choose us
We were voted ‘Top Employer for Graduates to Work for in the Energy & Utilities Sector’ 2023/24 by TheJobCrowd – the only ranking based on graduate reviews. The majority of our graduates decide to work for us because we offer them the option of choosing their own rotations, allowing the opportunity to find out which business areas are best fit. At the same time, we allow you to live and work abroad, expand your intercultural skills and build up an internal global network.
