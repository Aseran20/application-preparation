# Tavily Research: Gunvor Group

## Search 1: Company Mission & Values

**Source:** Gunvor Group official materials and career page

**Key Findings:**

### Core Values:
- **Entrepreneurial mindset** - Emphasized in graduate program description as core requirement
- **Analytical rigor** - Seeking "analytical and driven individuals"
- **Commercial drive** - Focus on building commercial expertise

### Mission Statement:
"Creating logistics solutions that safely and efficiently move physical energy, bulk materials, and base metals from where they are sourced and stored to where they are demanded most."

### What They're Known For:
1. **One of the world's largest independent commodities trading houses** - Generated US $127 billion turnover on 177 million MT in 2023
2. **Global presence** - Operating in trading, shipping, refining, and investments across energy transition projects
3. **Strategic infrastructure investments** - Refineries, pipelines, storage, and terminals that complement core trading

### Sustainability Commitment:
- Committed to cut Scope 1 and 2 emissions by 40% by 2025
