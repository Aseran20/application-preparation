Job Description
 

The LGT Capital Partners Private Markets Analyst Program is a unique opportunity for recent graduates to launch an exciting career with a leading alternative investment specialist focused on private markets.
 

The Analyst Program is structured as a 2-year full-time employment with the opportunity for outstanding candidates to continue their career at LGT Capital Partners as an Associate. The position is located in Switzerland (near Zurich) and/or the United Kingdom (London).

The anticipated start date is 1 July 2026 and the Program starts with a 3-weeks tailored training module and includes continuous on-the-job training.

The team invests globally in private credit – one of the fastest-growing alternative asset classes - seeking to generate high levels of investment income from private credit direct/ co-investments, secondaries and fund investments. The team has a flexible mandate to find the most compelling risk adjusted returns across different credit-based sub-strategies, including direct lending, credit opportunities, CLOs, NPLs, asset-backed credit, and non-correlated strategies and invests into the asset class on behalf of a diverse global client base.
 

The Analyst joining the Private Credit Solutions team will focus on developing the following core investment skills:

Support and take ownership in the Private Credit investment process, incl. sourcing, screening, due diligence, execution and monitoring of Private Credit co-investments into private loans alongside private credit managers, secondary transactions of limited partner interests in private credit funds as well as fund selection;
Coverage of investment universe, including regular interaction with various counterparts such as private credit managers and intermediaries, sector analysis and research;
Support and enhance credit analysis and modelling, transaction underwriting and execution;
Support in preparing documentation for the investment committee and client presentations;
Monitor and analyze existing Private Credit investments and market trends including regular maintenance and development of internal databases;
Support overall Private Credit Investment Team and contribute to overall development of the multi-manager Private Credit practice.
Requirements

Outstanding academic credentials from a leading university at Bachelor or Master level in business, economics, finance, financial engineering, accounting, or similar subject;
Interest in investing and willingness to learn;
Solid understanding of financial statements and fundamental business analysis;
Capable to analyze and draw conclusions from sizeable data sets, with help of your modeling skills;
Ability to collaborate effectively with a diverse team, with excellent written and oral English skills – other languages are a plus;
Enjoying work in an entrepreneurial atmosphere, with curiosity and a good flair for organization.
