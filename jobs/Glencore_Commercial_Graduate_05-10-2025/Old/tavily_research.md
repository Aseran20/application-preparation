## Search 2: Recent Company News (2025)

**Query**: "Glencore news 2025"

### Key Findings:

**Recent Strategic Moves**:
1. **Nickel operations expansion**: Glencore emerged as potential buyer for BHP's Nickel West assets, demonstrating strategic positioning in critical metals
2. **Copper production targets**: Company targeting to return to 1 million tons of copper annually by 2028, with $13B in new copper projects in Argentina
3. **Technology partnerships**: Executed MOU with Metallium for metal recovery operations in Texas (Horne Smelter partnership)

**Market Challenges**:
- Navigating commodity volatility (coal earnings tumbled due to supply/demand dynamics)
- Investor pressure to demonstrate copper production growth
- Strategic exits of senior trading personnel (but not relevant for grad programme)

**Strategic Direction**:
- Heavy focus on copper and critical metals for energy transition
- Balancing traditional commodities with decarbonization commitments
- Expanding in North America and Argentina
