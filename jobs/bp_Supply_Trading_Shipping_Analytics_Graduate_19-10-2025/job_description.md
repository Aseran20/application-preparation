Our Supply, Trading & Shipping (ST&S) Graduate Programme

Early careers who join the Supply, Trading & Shipping early careers programme will follow one of these tracks: trading, analytics and commercial. Early careers will follow a structured development offer to accelerate the acquisition of foundational technical skills, experience and knowledge; and application in the business world. The programme supports transition from university to discipline professional and provides a framework of ‘non-technical’ skills to support building personal effectiveness skills.

Supply, Trading & shipping development track and career pathway: 

Analytics track

Analysts have an opportunity to directly influence the bottom line as part of a highly commercial and skilled team. Working in the fast-paced world of our trading business and with access to vast amounts of data and information, you’ll focus on rotations that are designed to build your skills and knowledge to drive continued growth in trading profits and acting as a subject matter expert on energy market fundamentals. 

ST&S Analytics includes a range of disciplines, some of these are mentioned below:

Short term market analytics (Strategists): seeks to identify and understand fundamental drivers that influence market prices up to two years forward. Analysts are often co-located on the trading desks, and employ models of supply and demand, physical storage, weather, shipping, and power generation, coupled with real-time data feeds, to provide actionable commercial insights to the front-line trading teams.
Fundamentals Modelling & Innovation: We spearhead our digital ambition, advancing our fundamental modelling capabilities, leveraging AI and Machine Learning to generate data driven insights, increase automation, and delivered in line with the trading bench / discipline strategies.
Strategic insights: Identifying high margin deployment of capital expenditure, offtake and sale decisions and highlighting investment and trading opportunities for T&S and bp in incubatory and established energy markets.
Structuring: works closely with the trading and origination teams to structure and value complex financial and physical transactions. The modelling of key drivers of financial value, deal scenario analysis and designing tailored customer solutions are a strong focus for the team.
