Team presentation

The Secondaries & Mandates team offers a comprehensive range of private equity solutions tailored to the needs of our institutional and private clients.

For over 20 years, we have been focusing on delivering strong returns to our investors by leveraging a collaborative approach across three investment strategies: primary fund investments, secondary transactions, and co-investments.

We currently manage €6bn in assets under management and we have committed €5.7bn across these 3 strategies since our first investment in 2001.

Through our secondary activity, we provide bespoke investment solutions for investors and general partners seeking liquidity or new options for their private equity assets. This includes acquiring high-quality mid- market private companies on the secondary market, as well as fund interests managed by leading private equity firms.

Our investment team consists of 13 investment professionals based in Paris.

Within the Secondaries & Mandates team you will be actively involved in all stages of the secondary investment process, including sourcing, analyzing, executing, and monitoring secondary transactions.

This position offers a unique opportunity to join a market-leading private equity platform and gain substantial market and investment exposure to one of the fastest-growing asset classes in the private equity industry.
